#+title: Timestamps

* Active timestamps
<2024-01-15 Mon>
<2024-01-15 Mon 10:30>

* Inactive timestamps
[2024-01-15 Mon]
[2024-01-15 Mon 10:30]

* Date ranges
<2024-01-15>--<2024-01-20>

* Time ranges
<2024-01-15 Mon 10:00-11:00>

* Repeaters
<2024-01-15 Mon +1w>
<2024-01-15 Mon .+1m>

* Warning period
DEADLINE: <2024-01-15 Mon -3d>
